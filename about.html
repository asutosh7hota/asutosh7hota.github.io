Something more about me!

I am pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from the College of Engineering and Technology. I have been an integral part f the college's technical society Zairza I love binge watching and love cooking in my free time. Other than that I sketch caricatures and love reading.I follow tech blogs as well as codecamps and conferences on youtube. I started my journey into the opensource last winters and I must admit it has been a great experience. Currently I am focusing to get better at understanding the concepts of deep learning and neural nets, audited two courses CS229 and CS231n. I have been referring concepts from ISLR as it is a great read! Apart from that Andrej Karpathy has been a inspiration for the quest in machine learning and his blog seems a must read for enthusiasts in this field! It's always fun watching Siraj Raval's awesome videos! Exploring more of data science each new day!

I have been associated with a few great folks around my city.I am a lead volunteer at Google Developers Group Bhubaneswar. We organise meetups and events that encourages folks to join the opensource community and spread the love of computer science. Also have joined the Engineers without borders, Bhubaneswar subsection which aims to improve the quality of lives of people who need the bliss of technology to improve their standard of living, especially improving awareness and education.
